FAQ


Can I remove my name from Debate Scout without the addon?

In general cases - no. The only time where we'll take your name off of listings is if you're in a financial position in which you're unable to pay for the addon. In other cases, we can't remove your name for free because of 3 reasons. First, to ensure the integrity of this service, we have to construct some kind of a deterrant to removing your name from the listings. If we didn't, no one would be listed here. Second, with a site full of debaters around the country, privacy and strategy are indistinguishable. Third, we compile this information from a multitude of sites, all of which are public. That means that even if you remove your listing from Debate Scout because of privacy reasons, your information will still be available on other sites.

In the case of privacy, we'd suggest contacting the websites that we compile information from. Once you remove your information from those websites, contact us and we'll make sure it's removed from our website.
